# Contributing to Tagzeit

Thanks for your interest! A few things to know:

## ⚠️ Disclaimers

- **This is experimental research software.** It is developed on a specific local environment (Mac M-series) and we cannot guarantee it will work on your system or setup.
- **No support guarantees.** We'll do our best to review PRs and issues, but this is a learning project, not a production service.
- **Results may vary.** Model training is non-deterministic even with seed control. Your outputs may differ from ours.

## Basics

1. Fork the repo and create a feature branch.
2. Keep changes focused — one concern per PR.
3. If you change data generation or training, include a brief note on what you tested and what results you observed.

## Issues

Use the standard issue templates provided in `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/`. For bugs, include your environment details (OS, Python version, hardware).
